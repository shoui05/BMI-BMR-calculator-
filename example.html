Balanced Nutrition
Focus on whole foods: vegetables, lean proteins, complex carbs, and healthy fats. Avoid ultra-processed foods as much as possible.
💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses (2L) of water daily. Increase intake during exercise or in hot weather to stay properly hydrated.
😴
Quality Sleep
Aim for 7–9 hours per night. Poor sleep disrupts hunger hormones like ghrelin and leptin, leading to overeating and slower metabolism.
🏃
Regular Exercise
Combine cardio (150 min/week) with strength training (2–3x/week) for optimal health, fat loss, and muscle preservation.
🧘
Manage Stress
Chronic stress elevates cortisol, which promotes fat storage especially around the belly. Practice mindfulness, yoga, or deep breathing daily.
📏
Track Progress
Monitor your weight weekly, not daily. Day-to-day fluctuations are normal. Focus on long-term trends over several weeks for accurate insight.
