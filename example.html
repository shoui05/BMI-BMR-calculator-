📐 BMI Formulas Used

Balanced Nutrition
Fill half your plate with vegetables, a quarter with lean protein, and a quarter with complex carbs. Prioritize whole, unprocessed foods and limit added sugars.
Diet
💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink 30–35 ml of water per kg of body weight daily. Start your morning with 500ml before coffee. Signs of dehydration include dark urine, fatigue, and headaches.
Hydration
😴
Quality Sleep
7–9 hours per night is optimal for most adults. Poor sleep disrupts ghrelin and leptin (hunger hormones), increases cortisol, and slows muscle recovery by up to 40%.
Recovery
🏋️
Progressive Training
Aim for 150 min/week of moderate cardio + 2–3 strength sessions. Progressive overload — gradually increasing resistance — is the #1 driver of muscle growth and fat loss.
Exercise
🧘
Manage Stress
Chronic stress elevates cortisol, which promotes visceral fat storage and muscle breakdown. Practice deep breathing (4-7-8 method), meditation, or journaling for 10 min daily.
Mental Health
📏
Track Consistently
Weigh yourself weekly (same time, morning, fasted). Focus on 4-week trends, not daily fluctuations. Track food for at least 2 weeks to understand your actual caloric intake.
